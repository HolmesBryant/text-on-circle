Text on circle component

Renders text on a circle.

Attributes

  • fill [optional]: The color of the inside of the circle. "none" for transparent.
  • stroke [optional]: The color of the line around the circle. "none" for transparent.
  • text [optional]: The text to place on the circle. This is used primarily for dynamically changing the test with javascript
    • example: elem.setAttribute('text', 'Some other text.');

Usage

<script type="module" src="text-on-circle.js"></script>
<text-on-circle>Some Text on a Circle</text-on-circle>
